About GreenSteps

Our Mission

In a world where climate change and environmental degradation are growing concerns, **GreenSteps** is designed to empower individuals to take meaningful action. We believe that even the smallest steps, like reducing plastic waste, saving water, or conserving electricity, can contribute to a healthier planet. Our platform provides an easy and interactive way for users to track their eco-friendly habits and stay motivated in their sustainability journey.

At **GreenSteps**, we aim to build a community of environmentally conscious individuals who inspire and support each other. Through challenges, leaderboards, and insightful tips, we make green living engaging and rewarding.
